💸 we're sharing more revenue with you

Flipping the script on thirdweb Pay.

   

We’re excited to announce we’re sharing more revenue with developers.

For every transaction on your app, 70% of the fee is now sent directly to your wallet.

💸 In other words for every $1k in transaction fees, you make $700.

Here’s how it works:

thirdweb collects a 1% fee per end-user transaction, and 70% of these fees are shared with you and instantly transferred to your wallet.

Example: If a user purchases $100 worth of Polygon through your application, thirdweb charges a total fee of $1.00. Of this, thirdweb collects $0.30 and sends $0.70 to you.

There are a number of ways to generate revenue for your app:

◆ Top-up: Have users add funds to their wallet
◆ Commerce: Take payments in crypto for off-chain assets
◆ Transactions: Help users get crypto for any contract interaction

Check out the demo to see these in action.

We believe that founders should have multiple revenue streams for their app or game — Developers can now generate revenue for their app whenever an end-user completes a transaction.
