Welcome to YieldFest, a celebration of the new yield sources being integrated into the Hyperdrive Protocol!

Over the coming weeks, you can expect to see additional yield sources such as LRTs, Morpho vaults, AAVE markets, and many more that will open up fresh opportunities for generating returns on your assets with fixed and variable rates—all within your control.

Our team at DELV believes in empowering the DeFi space and we want to make sure liquidity providers, yield farmers, and traders have the knowledge and freedom to take the approach they feel is best for them. And we’re always on the lookout for ways to support the broader DeFi community!

Connecting the Dots

The Hyperdrive Protocol was created by DELV. We launched an instance of the Hyperdrive Protocol to mainnet on behalf of the Element DAO with two initial yield sources—sDAI and stETH. The Element DAO recently passed EGP-34, which engaged DELV as a non-exclusive service provider to start onboarding additional yield sources and deploy to new chains and L2s. (To be clear, other companies may seek to deploy yield sources, just as DELV may implement Hyperdrive for other non-Element DAO projects.)

We’re excited to be selected by the Element DAO to give Hyperdrive users the chance to explore innovative yield opportunities while providing more options to pursue the financial strategies they want. 

Come along on the YieldFest journey, help us spread the word, and get ready to dive into a world of new yield!
