Face-to-face, IRL interactions leave more lasting impressions and – in our experience – create stronger teams. By leveraging the power of IRL events, the Solana community has been able to empower thousands of builders within the ecosystem, providing them with all the necessary support needed to keep shipping. Here's a few IRL opportunities coming up:

 

  • @ThePortMiami is coming! The Port, located in Wynwood, Miami, is a co-working hub for Solana builders to come together and collaborate. If you’re in Miami, get ready. The ship is about to sail later this month. 
  • @SuperteamAE presents the Founders’ Villa Season 2, happening in Dubai this October. Founders are invited for an exciting 3-week program taking place from Oct. 6-26th, where they'll get the opportunity to brainstorm with other founders, pitch their startups to VCs, and compete for cash prizes. Get more details and register here. 
  • Have you heard about what's happening in Salt Lake City this month? mtnDAO v6 has begun! If you're a Solana developer or a web2 dev looking for support, like-minded discussions, and collaboration, mtnDAO is where you should be. Learn more about it here.

If you’re in a city and there’s not a Solana event happening there — that means it’s up to YOU to start it! Head to River to suggest your own meetup.

Build, build, build in the Global PYUSD Portals Hackathon!

@portal_hq partnered with @PayPal to launch the Global PYUSD Portal Hackathon! This 5-week, online event is aimed at encouraging Solana developers to build projects using PYUSD. Registration is now open and closes on Aug. 19, 2024.

 

Winning projects get a chance at PYUSD awards as well as a sponsored trip and a showcase of the top two projects at the Solana Hacker House in Hong Kong this October. Interested participants can register here.
