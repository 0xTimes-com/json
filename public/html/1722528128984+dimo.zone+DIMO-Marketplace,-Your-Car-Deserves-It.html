AN APP STORE FOR THE MODERN VEHICLE

The DIMO Marketplace is a useful hub of vehicle services at the heart of the DIMO ecosystem, designed to rethink the way we experience vehicle ownership and data utilization. The iPhone experienced widespread adoption and an explosion of functionality once the app store was introduced, and allowed third-party developers to build incredible features for a powerful hardware device. DIMO is creating an app store for your car, opening the floodgates for applications to be built on top of the wealth of data produced by vehicles around the globe. 

Marble

Marble is an insurance policy aggregator that acts as a smart agent, scanning all available options to get you the best price possible based on the data from your vehicle. 

RepairPal

RepairPal is the leading provider of auto repair and maintenance information on the web. Triage your car's issue, and find a certified repair shop to get back on the highway asap. 

Caribou

Caribou is a vehicle financing platform whose mission is to put drivers in control of their auto finances.

Caribou partners with trusted lenders to save customers hundreds of dollars on their car payments. 

The Carbon Offset Company

DIMO and The Carbon Offset Company have partnered to give car owners a simple way to track and then offset their personal carbon emissions.

They take a holistic and verified approach to carbon offsetting through straightforward tree planting initiatives
