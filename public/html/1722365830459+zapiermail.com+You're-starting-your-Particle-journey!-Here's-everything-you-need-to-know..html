Our flagship product: Modular Smart Wallet-as-a-Service

Particle's Modular Smart Wallet-as-a-Service (WaaS) tools allow developers to tap into MPC-TSS and social logins to enable self-custodial, dApp-embedded wallets accessible through users' Web2 accounts. It also empowers them to natively leverage the potential of ERC-4337 account abstraction, enabling a seamless, flexible experience.

Our ultimate goal?  To elevate users' experience within dApps while simplifying the task of building them, paving the way for mass Web3 adoption.

The Particle ecosystem, at a glance

An image says more than a thousand words, so we've condensed the whole Particle ecosystem into one:

In short, we're revamping the Particle ecosystem to become the Intent-Centric Modular Access Layer of the Open Web. The Particle ecosystem features:

  • Modular Smart Wallet-as-a-Service, simplifying users' interactions within dApps and allowing developers to tap into ERC-4337 Account Abstraction seamlessly.
  • A Confidential zkStack, providing users with full identity and transactional privacy.
  • The Intent Fusion Protocol, a framework for users to express their goals, in-dApp, outsourcing their execution and radically simplifying user experience in Web3.

These pillars are supported by:

  • Omnichain Account Abstraction, a proprietary technology enabling cross-chain account abstraction and asset flow.
  • The Particle Network Token, a mechanism to capture and distribute the network's value among stakeholders while incentivizing participants.
  • The Particle Chain: An independent zkEVM powering the above features.

About Particle Network

Particle Network is building the Intent-Centric Access Layer of Web3. Particle's Modular Smart Wallet-as-a-Service tools allow developers to tap into MPC-TSS and social logins to enable self-custodial, dApp-embedded wallets accessible through users' Web2 accounts.
