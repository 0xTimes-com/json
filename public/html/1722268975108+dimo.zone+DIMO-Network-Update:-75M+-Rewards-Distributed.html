Connecting to the DIMO Network is quick and easy

If your car already has an app (like the Tesla app, Fordpass, or My BMW), you can get up and running in minutes using the software connection. Otherwise, DriveDIMO offers two different hardware devices that plug into the OBD2 port that is standard in every vehicle 2008 and newer. 

Don’t wait to connect your car — every week you delay, you’re missing out on a chunk of rewards and access to a broader ecosystem of apps.

 

DIMO baseline rewards are distributed weekly at 5am UTC and will be displayed in the Market tab of DIMO Mobile.
