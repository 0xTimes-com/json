₿ Signifcant milestone in the world of on-chain SNARK verification

For the first time in history, it’s now possible to interactively verify a SNARK proof on Bitcoin mainnet using BitVMX. And unlike different proposals that require OP_CAT, this verification method does not require any change in the Bitcoin protocol.

Read the details here.
